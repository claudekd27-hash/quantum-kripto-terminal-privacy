# Quantum Kripto Terminal – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Quantum Kripto Terminal (“the App”) is a cryptocurrency market tracking and technical analysis application designed to provide informational market data, portfolio monitoring, news feeds, and economic calendar features.

---

## Information Collection

Quantum Kripto Terminal does not require user registration and does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Government identification
- Payment information

The application may locally store user-created data on the device, including:

- Portfolio entries
- Favorite coins
- Alarm settings
- User preferences

This data is stored locally on the user's device and is not transmitted to external servers owned by the developer.

---

## Third-Party Services

The App retrieves market and financial data from third-party cryptocurrency and financial APIs, including but not limited to:

- MEXC Exchange
- News RSS providers
- Economic calendar providers
- Cloudflare Workers infrastructure

These services may temporarily process technical request data such as:

- IP address
- Device request headers
- Request timestamps

This processing is handled by the respective third-party providers according to their own privacy policies.

---

## Analytics and Advertising

Currently, Quantum Kripto Terminal does not use:

- User analytics platforms
- Behavioral tracking
- Personalized advertising
- Account profiling

If advertising or analytics services are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

---

## Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to help protect application stability and prevent abuse of API infrastructure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

---

## Financial Disclaimer

Quantum Kripto Terminal is provided for informational and educational purposes only.

The App does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, brokerage services, or guarantees of profit. Users are solely responsible for their own investment decisions.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
